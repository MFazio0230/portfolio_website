BOOMBOX

Relieve the magic of the 1980s with the music that shaped a generation. Introducing the ultimate retro music media player, BOOMBOX -- where music and memories come alive.

Research

Whether users have directly experienced the 1980’s or not, there are always certain expectations from them when it comes to products of nostalgia. With that in mind, for the app to be successful by any measure, it was of crucial importance to embody the distinctly recognizable patterns and colors of the decade.

Ideation

These days, almost all smart phone users are familiar with music media players. Because of this, there was no need to overcomplicate an already efficient and widely used user flow model.

Wireframes

It was important to give listeners an interface loaded with all the popular music genres of the decade while at the same time offering them a few random suggestions with an option to view a larger selection of songs within the genre. All this, without confusing the user with sensory overload. So, to accomplish this, vertical and horizontal scrolling with a see more option is affectively implemented on both the search and search results pages alike.

Design

The art and style of the 1980’s is iconic--making it instantly recognizable. The colors most often associated with the decade are those of high contrast and vibrancy--with fonts pairings following a similar pattern of high contrast.

Final Product

Step into the nostalgic and immersive world of BOOMBOX -- the music-video mobile app that takes you back to the iconic era of the 1980’s.
